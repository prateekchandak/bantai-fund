Why Now

The
inflection

Three structural forces converging to make AI infrastructure the most consequential investment theme of the decade.

01 — Demand

Compute demand is compounding at an impossible rate

AI training compute requirements double every six months. Every new foundation model generation demands an order-of-magnitude more than the last. Today's infrastructure cannot serve tomorrow's workloads.

02 — Supply

Supply chains are being rebuilt from first principles

Geopolitical fractures have exposed critical single points of failure in the AI supply chain. The next decade is defined by who builds domestic capability in advanced packaging, memory, and energy — creating category-defining companies.

03 — Economics

Infrastructure compounds — winners take everything

Infrastructure markets are winner-take-most. The company that owns a layer of the AI stack in 2026 will compound that position for 20 years. The window for early-stage entry at each layer is closing fast.
